Mississippi River Restoration Foundation

About

stablished in 1990, the Mississippi River Restoration Foundation has been a leading advocate for the protection and restoration of the Mississippi River for over three decades.

Volunteer Opportunities

River Cleanup Volunteer: Join us in removing litter and debris from the river and its banks to improve water quality and habitat conditions. Habitat Restoration Volunteer: Assist our team in planting native vegetation, restoring wetlands, and implementing erosion control measures to enhance riverbank stability and wildlife habitat.
